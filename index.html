Project on Console

You can download Visual Studio on this link: https://code.visualstudio.com/ When you are there, click on download. When it's done downloading, click it and keep clicking next then click install.

The console screen is where your outputs go. If there is anything wrong with your code, it will show what is wrong. This is helpful for when you're coding or testing and something looks wrong. On the bottom of the visual studio screen, you have 4 tabs. Click output and you will see your console. There is a blue bar at the very bottom of the window. In the blue bar, there is a X symbol and a warning symbol on the left of the blue bar. If the numbers beside it are 0, you have no problems in your code, if it's not you have a problem(s) in your code. To "print" something in the console, type console.log() and in the brackets, type what what you want to put in the console.
